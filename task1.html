Real estate encompasses the buying, selling, and development of land and buildings. It is a significant sector in the global economy, influencing everything from individual wealth to urban development. Residential real estate deals with homes and living spaces, while commercial real estate involves properties used for business purposes, such as offices, shops, and factories. The market is driven by various factors including economic conditions, interest rates, and government policies. Real estate investments are often considered stable and profitable, providing opportunities for rental income and property appreciation. In recent years, technological advancements and changing consumer preferences have reshaped the industry, emphasizing sustainable practices and smart home technologies. Real estate professionals, including agents, brokers, and developers, play crucial roles in navigating this complex and dynamic market.
